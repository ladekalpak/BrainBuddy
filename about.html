About Brain Buddy

Brain Buddy is a Smart School Tutor platform designed for Classes 8 to 12. Our goal is to bridge the gap between students and quality educators by providing flexible, affordable, and transparent learning opportunities online.

Students can connect directly with trusted teachers, book learning sessions, and benefit from a transparent rating and review system. Teachers gain exposure, career growth, and flexibility, while institutions can easily hire top-rated educators.

👨‍🏫 Teacher Profiles

Educators create professional profiles with qualifications, experience, and teaching styles.

📚 Student Access

Students browse teachers, filter by subject, ratings, and pricing, then book slots instantly.

⭐ Ratings & Reviews

Transparent rating-driven system ensures high-quality teaching and student satisfaction.

🏫 Institutional Support

Schools and colleges can hire top-performing teachers based on performance data.

Our Vision

We aim to redefine the teaching-learning model by introducing transparency, flexibility, and accessibility in online education. By maintaining affordable pricing and offering free student access, Brain Buddy promotes educational equity while opening new career opportunities for teachers worldwide.
